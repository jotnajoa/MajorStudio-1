Human Studies
Film Archive
HSFA collections comprise more than 7 million feet of film and almost one thousand hours of video recordings. These visual research resources, along with related documentary material encompass a broad range of documentary, travelogue, ethnographic and amateur genres dating from 1908 to the present.
In this project, the archival material from 1950s to 2010s is illustrated to show where the archival material belongs to.
